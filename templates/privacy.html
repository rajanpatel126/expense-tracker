Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we collect the expense data you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or any financial account credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and enabling filters. We do not use your financial data for advertising, analytics sold to third parties, or any purpose beyond operating Spendly for you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorized access. Your account password is stored as a one-way hash and is never readable by us. You may delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove all associated data.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third parties. We may use infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the platform, but these providers do not have access to your expense data for their own purposes and are bound by confidentiality obligations.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided on the site. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly.
